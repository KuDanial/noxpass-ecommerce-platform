PRIVACY POLICY

Last Updated: December 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to NoxPass. This privacy policy explains how our group, DIZASS, collects and uses your information when you visit our website or subscribe to our PC game service. We respect your privacy and want to keep your data safe.

2. Information We Collect

When you register or use NoxPass, we may collect the following info:

  • Personal Info: Username, email address, and password.
  • Payment Info: We do not store your credit card details directly. Payments are handled by secure gateways like Stripe or PayPal.
  • Gaming Data: Which games you download and play (so our AI can recommend better games for you).

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the data we collect to:

  • Process your subscription and allow game downloads.
  • Improve our website and game catalog.
  • Send you emails about new games or special discounts (only if you agree).
  • Detect and prevent fraud or account sharing issues.

4. Sharing Your Information

We strictly do not sell your personal data to anyone. We only share data with:

  • Service Providers: Like our payment processors to handle transactions.
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or university regulations (since this is a student project).

5. Security

We use security measures like password encryption and secure servers (Microsoft Azure) to protect your account. However, please keep your password secret and do not share it with others.

6. Cookies

Our website uses simple cookies to remember your login status so you don't have to sign in every time you visit.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this policy, please contact the DIZASS team via our Support page or email us at support@noxpass.uitm.edu.my.
