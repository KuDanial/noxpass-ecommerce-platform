HOW CAN WE HELP?

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel anytime by going to your Account Dashboard > Subscription Settings. You will still have access until the end of your billing month.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit/debit cards via Stripe, PayPal, and online banking (FPX).

My game download is stuck. What do I do?

Please check your internet connection and ensure you have enough storage space. If the problem persists, try restarting the NoxPass launcher.

Can I share my account?

Account sharing is only allowed on the Ultimate Plan. Basic Plan users are restricted to one active device at a time.
